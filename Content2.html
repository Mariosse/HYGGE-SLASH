MICROTREND:

Coloured glass

Coloured glass décor. From HAY to Hübsch, we’re seeing this micro trend everywhere. Maybe it’s the retro 70’s feel. Maybe it’s the fact that a pop of colour is like a breath of fresh air among the endless conveyor belt of neutral colours which dominate the Scandinavian interior scene. Either way, we’re loving it.


WORDS: MARISSA RODRIGUEZ SCHIMIZZI
