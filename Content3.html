"The Cloud" Mirror

A step-by-step guide to creating this on-trend clay mirror. Hang it in your bedroom, your bathroom...anywhere really!


WORDS & PHOTOGRAPHY:
MARISSA RODRIGUEZ SCHIMIZZI
OCTOBER 1 2021

With just a few items easily found in hobby stores, art & craft shops or similar (hello: Søstrene Grene!) you can turn a boring mirror into a statement piece which shouts cute. Style it to your taste and choose a clay that matches your room, or go all out and create a colour pop with a statement colour!


Step 1: Gather your materials:
We went straight to a hobby store to get most of our materials (PANDURO). But be sure to check out stores like Søstrene Grene, Flying Tiger, Silvan and second hand stores for cheap DIY materials.
Step 2: Trace your mirror
Grab some baking paper and trace around the edge of your mirror. This will be your guide for making all those funky clay curves!
Step 3: Roll your clay
We used white, pink and blue clay, but you can use whatever combo you like! When you have a long enough roll, arrange it into a 'cloud' shape on your pre-traced baking paper.
Step 4: Bake or air dry your clay
Depending on what type of clay you buy, you will have to either bake it or let it dry. We used FIMO clay which needs to be baked at 110˚ for 30 minutes. Airdry clay needs to be left overnight or longer.
Step 5: Embellish
This step is optional, but if you want to add some extra pizzazz to you mirror, you can add some paint. Grab a paintbrush and some watered down acrylic paint and flick the paint all over the clay to create this speckled effect.
Step 6: Attach to your mirror
Now grab your hot glue gun to attach your clay ‘cloud’ to your mirror. It’s better to use tiny blobs dotted all over the back – that way you won’t be able to see any glue from the front.


And VOILÀ! Your cloud mirror is ready to hang!
